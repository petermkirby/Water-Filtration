North Cohasset

What Weir River water testing means for North Cohasset

The North Cohasset section of Cohasset draws from the same Weir River Water System as Hingham — so the same EPA testing data applies to your household too.

Why this affects North Cohasset, not just Hingham

North Cohasset — the part of the Town of Cohasset closest to Hingham — doesn't draw from a separate Cohasset water system for this service area. Along with Hingham and Hull, it's supplied by the Weir River Water System, operated by Aquarion Water Company from sources in Hingham — Accord Brook, Accord Pond, and eleven groundwater wells across the Weir Watershed. Whatever shows up in EPA testing of the Weir River system's source water and treatment process is what actually reaches North Cohasset taps, regardless of town lines.

Hingham Water Watch was started to track that data in plain language. We're a Hingham-based volunteer effort, but since the water supply is shared, so is the relevant data.
