Get tested

Request a free at-home water test

System-wide reports only tell part of the story. A household-level test accounts for your specific plumbing, service line, and fixtures.

What to expect

No cost, no obligation. This is a volunteer-run community service, not a sales call.

A local volunteer follows up within a few days to schedule a convenient time.

We test for the same categories flagged in EPA monitoring: PFAS, lead, and common disinfection byproducts.

You'll get a plain-language explanation of your results, and, if anything stands out, a rundown of reasonable next steps for your household.

Hingham Water Watch is an independent, volunteer-run community water quality initiative. We are not affiliated with the Town of Hingham, the Hingham Water Department, or Aquarion Water Company. Data referenced on this site is drawn from public EPA and Massachusetts DEP records.
